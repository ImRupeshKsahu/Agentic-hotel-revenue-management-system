-> ADR (Average Daily Rate)

Daily ADR = Sum of Rates for all Guests staying that night / Total Guests staying that night

How to get this from transactions data - "unroll" the bookings so that Guest A appears on July 1, July 2, and July 3 if he had booked for these three nights. Then, for every specific calendar date, we took the Average of the price paid by everyone staying that night.

-> Occupancy Rate

Occupancy Rate = Rooms Sold / Total Rooms Available

-> RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room) = Occupacy Rate * ADR
   Combines price + demand

-> Competitor Rate is competitor's ADR
